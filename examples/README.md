# Examples

These are illustrative walkthroughs, not real client data. Each is a fictional composite that shows the shape of a Quorum run: the interview, the seated board, the audit map, a few findings, a scored backlog, and the framework left running. Numbers in brackets are placeholders.

The examples use function-labeled lenses (a positioning lens, a retention lens) rather than named experts, so nothing here implies any real person reviewed or endorsed anything. In a real run, you seat named experts who clear the Faithful Representation Gate.

- `example-b2b-saas.md` - a two-person B2B SaaS, post first sale, no roadmap.
- `example-solo-creator.md` - a solo education creator with an audience and one paid product.
- `example-service-business.md` - a local service business going from side income to full-time.
